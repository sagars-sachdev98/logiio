What does Logiio Solve?

Logiio is a mobile application that is designed especially for you to bring unique solutions and fulfillment of the orders, assuring great quality, speed, and reasonable cost. It has been built with keeping end-to-end logistics in mind and it aims to provide various unique solutions for all the right needs of the pickup and delivery of a courier that simplifies your life and makes it easier for on-the-go orders and delivery of parcels.

Logiio combines people, processes, and technology into one system to get the best results from all sources. The ultimate objective is to get maximum value from every division within the business while providing the highest customer satisfaction.

Logiio saves your time by reducing the paperwork and saving order-related details. It also helps for live tracking of orders.

What does Logiio do?

Logiio provides end-to-end delivery services. It helps to save time and hassle that comes with the parcel delivery of logistics. It has a tracking system that monitors the whereabouts of each parcel at all times according to your preferences, including orders and other related details. There is also an order management system for users who would like to track their orders.

Logiio helps in providing the best service by organizing and managing the whole unorganized sector of logistics. You just have to enter the weight, source, and destination of the parcel and it will automatically calculate the cost for your service.
